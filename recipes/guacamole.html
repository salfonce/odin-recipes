Simply Guacamole

Description

This is a recipe from allrecipes of a simple dish that can be used as a dip sauce for appetizers and snacks

Ingredients

  • 5 avocadoes - peeled and mashed
  • 2 tablespoon fresh lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup minced green onion
  • 1/2 cup minced fresh cilantro
  • salt and paper, to taste

Steps

  1. Stir together the avocadoes and lemon juice in a serving bowl
  2. Add the green onion and cilantro; mix well
  3. Season with salt and pepper
  4. Serve immediately or store covered in refrigerator with avocado pits in the bowl to keep from browning
