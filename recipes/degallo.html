Pico De Gallo de Alicia

Description

This is a recipe from allrecipes of an authentic Mexican salsa made with tomatoes, onions and jalapenos

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 red onion, finely chopped
  • 2 jalapeno pepper, seeded and chopped
  • 2 spring cilantro, finely chopped
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 1/2 small lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt to taste

Steps

  1. Mix tomato, red onion, jalapeno pepper,cilantro, garlic and lime juice in a bowl
  2. Season with garlic, salt and stir
  3. Cover bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate at least 4 hours
